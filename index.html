Secret Agent

Vestia Zeta

ベスティア・ゼータ

She is the mysterious 7th recruit of The Secret Archive Unit that has a full license to collect virtual data. Despite her being a promising rookie, who is also known as codename V.7, she turns clumsy when things are not going exactly according to the manual. It was initially a lonely mission to infiltrate the virtual world, but now she had found comfort here.
